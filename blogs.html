COVID-19: Skills for the Future Workplace

Posted on Tuesday 4 August 2020

Topics: Campus Life, Information Technology

The job market is changing post-pandemic, with increased demand for soft skills like adaptability, leadership, communication, and problem-solving. In addition to these, technology skills such as coding, data literacy, and knowledge of cloud and blockchain are becoming essential.

Platforms like Coursera, edX, and Udemy can help graduates build these skills.

Why Now is the Time to Shift Focus on Remote Learning

Posted on Wednesday 6 May 2020

Topics: Campus Life

The pandemic forced schools and universities to shift quickly to online learning, using tools like Google Classroom, Zoom, and MOOCs. While this digital shift presents challenges, it also offers opportunities for long-term educational innovation.

Hybrid learning models may become the new norm, providing affordable and flexible options for students worldwide.
