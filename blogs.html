COVID-19: Skills for the Future Workplace

Posted on Tuesday 4 August 2020

Topics: Campus Life, Information Technology

The job market is changing post-pandemic, with increased demand for soft skills like adaptability, leadership, communication, and problem-solving. In addition to these, technology skills such as coding, data literacy, and knowledge of cloud and blockchain are becoming essential. Virtual internships and online learning platforms such as Coursera, edX, and Udemy can help graduates build these skills from home.

Graduates should showcase transferable skills through projects and tailor their resumes to highlight relevant knowledge for the evolving workplace.

Why Now is the Time to Shift Focus on Remote Learning

Posted on Wednesday 6 May 2020

Topics: Campus Life

The pandemic forced schools and universities to shift quickly to online learning, using tools like Google Classroom, Zoom, and MOOC platforms. While this digital shift has challenges such as maintaining student attention and delivering practical lessons, it also presents an opportunity for long-term innovation.

Hybrid learning models combining in-person and online education may become the new norm, offering more affordable and flexible options for students worldwide.
